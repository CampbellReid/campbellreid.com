About Campbell

At just 22, Campbell Reid has transformed a childhood passion for programming into a burgeoning career poised to shape the future of software engineering. From creating Minecraft mods at 11 to excelling in Math, IT, and Physics, Campbell's journey is one of relentless pursuit of excellence. Currently in the final year at Griffith University on a scholarship, Campbell balances a stellar academic record with practical experience as a Junior Web Developer. Embedded Systems, Full Stack Development, and Algorithmic Design are not just skills but passions that drive him to push the boundaries of what's possible.
